The CTR-Test®

Der Caris Molecular Intelligence Test ist herkömmlichen Testverfahren überlegen. Der Grund liegt in der kombinierten Anwendung der verfügbaren Biomarker. So werden alle gängigen Korrelationen getestet, wo sonst beispielsweise nur nach BRAF Mutationen oder FISH geschaut wird, die immer nur einen isolierten Bereich abdecken. Somit wird sichergestellt, das möglichst alle verfügbaren Informationen, die im Tumorgewebe enthalten sind, ausgelesen werden. So ergibt sich ein vollständiges Bild, das für die Therapieauswahl entscheidend sein kann.

The Test Result for the Right Medication

The CTR-Test® uncovers which chemotherapeutic agents are ineffective for an individual patient with more than 95% accuracy.The result is clear and easy to interpret. It includes a diagram which shows the effect of the tested substances on the tumor cells. The result is interpreted according to the level of resistance:


  • Extreme Resistance (ER): Tumor cell growth could not be inhibited compared to an untreated control. Therapy failure is most likely to expect, if that drug is used.

  • Medium Resistance (MR): Tumor cell growth was only partly inhibited. There is an increased probability for therapy failure.

  • Slight Resistance (SR): Tumor cell growth was strongly inhibited. These chemotherapeutic agents are most likely to be effective.


It is usually possible to choose a number of drugs for a chemotherapeutic treatment. Medication depends on many factors: Type of tumor, origin and advance of the disease, number of prior treatments, side effects of other drugs and of course the individual condition of the patient as well as guidelines, study results and drug approval. Ineffective substances are excluded through the test. A more effective kind of treatment can be selected from the range of possible standard therapies. The CTR-Test® has the greatest benefit, if several standard therapies exist.

The CTR-Test® result shows the physician which chemotherapeutic agents are ineffective. Therefore he can administer an alternative and effective chemotherapy. If the patient is saved from unnecessary side effects, the quality of life can be improved.



Innovative Test Procedure for a Valid Result

After receipt of the tumor material, the CTR-Test® provides a valid result after only eight steps:

  • At first, fresh, alive tumor tissue is cut and digested by enzymes in order to obtain the tumor suspension with is necessary for analysis.

  • Tumor cells are kept under conditions, which favor growth of tumor cells and suppress growth of other, irritating cells.

  • Under these carefully controlled growth conditions, cells are exposed to the desired chemotherapeutic agents for five days.

  • Radioactively labeled thymidine is added to the cells for the last two days to be able to accurately determine cell growth later. Tumor cells grow differently strong during this period of time (extreme, medium and slight level of cell growth).

  • After these two days newly built, radioactively labeled DNA is obtained.

  • Radioactive signals are measured and used to determine cell growth.

  • The growth of cells treated with chemotherapeutic agents is compared to the growth of an untreated control group.

  • During the last step, the effectiveness of each drug is compared to those in the data base. In this way the degree of resistance is determined.


Highly reliable predictions of ineffectiveness of drugs distinguish the CTR-Test® from other testing procedures, which use alive tumor cells. Classification as ER (extreme resistance) is more than 95% accurate.
