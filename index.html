Who We Are

The Cancer Evaluation and Intelligence Center is founded to carry out the leading technologies to identify the treatments that fit most to an individual confronted with cancer. Officially accredited by the technology providers CEICs msission ist to deminish the boundaries for patients and physicians to apply personalized medicine.

What we do

CEIC first and foremost manages direct access to testing procedures that reveal the best available treatment options and potential studies globally. For our clients we overcome boundaries set by distance, availability, logistics and simply knowledge about available technologies.

Why we do it

Although it is already a common understanding that tumors vary in many ways, therapy decision are still mostly based on findings of general studies that do not take into count the individual nature of tumors. The result: treatments that have poor impact on tumor growth but sideeffects. CEIC tools help to identify the treatment options that fit.

FAQ

Your Partner for Best Care Management

Does it matter what kind of tumor I have?

The tests work on solid tumor diesease. That means all type of tumors that are clearly to identify by a mass. In contrary are forms of leukemia. Please cotatc us to check in your case.

What are my advantages to perform a test?

Cancer treatments are mostly based on studies and the conclusions out of the studies are converted into guidelines. Modern science already knows no tumor is a like. That simply means using a therapy that showed in a study it worked in a number of cases still does not mean it will work in your case. The tools ready to use predict evidence based the potential use out of a chemotherapy or identify available trageted therapies based on the specific condition of your tumor.

Should not everyone perform such a test?

Well, one of the golden rules in medicine and especially in oncology is: no case is alike. Healthcare providers stating it always makes sense nevertheless in which situation you are should alarm you. A diagnostic tool can only be as good as the therapies available. So before taking the decision for such a tool we look will take a close look on your case and connect you with an expert in short time.

My doctor has concerns to use such tools? Can we talk it over?

We support you at any stage. If your physician has concerns we can arrange a direct conversation with an expert of our partners so your physician and you get all questions answered.

Will I need a „go“ from my doctor?

In general: yes. The tools to identify an available treatment or even predict the response of a chemotherapy require tumor mass or other substances that contain tumor cells like pathology specimen that needs to be orderly send.

Do the tests argue against established guidelines?

The tests optimizing the process of decision taking within a cancer therapy. For example many chemo agents are available with no clear regime in benefit-risk ratio. So the tests help to identify the ones who are most likely not work on the tumor. Side effects can be avoided and time for agents is safed that due have impact on the tumor growth.
