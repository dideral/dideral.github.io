The Cancer Evaluation and Intelligence Center

CEIC

There are many developed therapy options in most cases when it comes to a cancer diagnose. But which one ist he right in your case? Doctors follow established guidelines to navigate the different options. In many cases, thats the right approach as the agents have shown impact in similiar cases. But what if not? And on which basis do we decide when there is no clear guideline regime anymore, the options are few? What if your cancer does not respond as estimated?

CEIC service is dedicated to offer high end diagnostics procedures to identify those treatment options that will most likely respond to your individual situation. With those diagnostics you tune the decision taking process from general data to individual (personalized) data. The diagnostics work on the info your cancer tell and allows precise counter interventions.


  • With the help of diagnostic procedures recommended and selected by us unnecessary treatments can be sorted out before started


  • The individual decision can be made in an evidence-based manner.


  • The best therapy for you is often able to increase survival time and to maintain quality of life.


  • You will receive a recommendation for a therapy which is tailored to your individual needs.


  • With CEIC you will get more than basic health care provides.


  • On request we can direct you to healthcare facilities with highest reputation

Our Service means we pick you up from second one. Please contact us so our patient service can provide you all information in your specfic case.
