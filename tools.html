The CTR Test

We have to distinguish two tools with different mode of operation:

The CTR test is predicting the estimated non-response on a chemeo therapy agent. This so called Chemotherapyresistance Test (CTR) is using vital tumor mass extracted in a surgery. With the results it becomes a guiding reference point on which of the available agnets the tumor will most likely not respond. So those agents who have a poor response rate can be sorted out and unnecessary side effects and useless chemocycles acan be avoided.

When is a CTR testing indicated:

Different Chemo agents are available with no clear alterating hirachy.

The guidelines do not offer any option. Mostly in recidive situations or tumors of unknown origin Tumors that are very rare often are not covered by guidelines due to a lack of scientific validation

General data of the test:
The Test is based on vital tumor speciman (approx. 1 gramm) that is extracted in a surgery.or from a maligne content.

 

  • More than 150 T applications performed


  • Accurancy of nearly 95% due to the use of vital tumor mass in contrast to dead tissue (like parrafin fixed pathology tissue)


  • The turn around time (time between specimen sending and available results) is about 7-9 days after received in the lab
